Projects

Selected people analytics and HR data science work.

These projects focus on workforce forecasting, attrition modeling, dashboard automation, and learning analytics for stakeholder decision-making.

Headcount Simulation Framework

Built a probabilistic and stochastic modeling framework to forecast workforce size and labor cost under attrition and transfer scenarios.

Tech: Python, statistical modeling, workforce data

Impact: Supports strategic workforce planning and labor cost forecasting.

Attrition Driver Analysis

Built attrition analysis models using Explainable Boosting Machines, logistic regression, and Bayesian approaches to surface actionable retention signals.

Tech: Python, EBM, logistic regression, Bayesian inference, feature engineering

Impact: Helps identify causal and interpretable retention factors for targeted action.

HR Data Automation And Dashboards

Built automated HR data processing and reporting workflows with dashboards for more reliable recurring workforce reporting.

Tech: Python, Power BI, HR datasets

Impact: Improved regular HR reporting and made workforce insights easier to share with stakeholders.

Learning Analytics

Analyzed more than 29,000 registrations and learning activities, plus post-survey impact analysis for learning teams.

Tech: Data analysis, survey analysis, learning data

Impact: Supported learning needs analysis and evaluation of learning content impact.
